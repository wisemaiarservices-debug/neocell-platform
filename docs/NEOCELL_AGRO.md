# NeoCell Agro

## Target Environment

Agrivoltaics, farms, SolarHub pilots, irrigation sites, and research centers.

## Purpose

Connect field data, solar assets, water systems, weather data, sensors, and gateways to NOVA OS.

## Connected Assets

- Solar arrays
- Inverters
- Batteries
- Pumps
- Irrigation valves
- Reservoirs
- Soil sensors
- Weather stations
- Field zones
- Gateways

## Demo Workflow

Heatwave forecast -> soil moisture decline -> irrigation demand forecast -> energy coordination -> operator recommendation -> measured impact.
