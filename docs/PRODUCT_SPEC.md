# NeoCell Product Specification

## Public Name

NOVA Infrastructure

## Internal Name

NeoCell

## Parent Platform

NOVA OS

## Mission

Connect deployment sites, gateways, sensor nodes, energy systems, water systems, communication status, maintenance events, and digital twin sync into one infrastructure module.

## Core Modules

- Deployments
- Gateways
- Sensor Nodes
- Energy Spine
- Water Spine
- Communications
- Edge Events
- Digital Twin Sync
- Maintenance
- Reports

## Product Variants

### NeoCell Agro

For agrivoltaics, farms, SolarHub pilots, and water-energy coordination.

### NeoCell North

For cold climates, remote sites, community resilience, and microgrid monitoring.

### NeoCell Desert

For hot climates, dust exposure, solar optimization, and water-scarce operations.

## MVP Success Metrics

- Deployment registered
- Gateway status tracked
- Sensor nodes registered
- Telemetry health visible
- Maintenance events tracked
- Digital Twin sync status visible
- SolarHub demo story supported
