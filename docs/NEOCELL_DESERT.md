# NeoCell Desert

## Target Environment

Middle East, North Africa, hot climates, desert sites, solar farms, and water-scarce environments.

## Purpose

Support monitoring for heat, dust, solar performance, water systems, and site readiness.

## Connected Assets

- Solar arrays
- Inverters
- Batteries
- Temperature sensors
- Water reservoirs
- Pumps
- Irrigation systems
- Weather stations
- Gateways

## Demo Workflow

Dust or heat risk -> solar efficiency forecast changes -> water-energy timing update -> operator recommendation -> impact tracking.
