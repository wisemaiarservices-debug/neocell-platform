# NeoCell SolarHub Positioning

## Title

NOVA Infrastructure powered by NeoCell

## SolarHub Fit

NeoCell shows how NOVA OS can connect to real-world renewable and agrivoltaic sites.

It supports:

- Deployment readiness
- Sensor integration
- Gateway status
- Renewable infrastructure monitoring
- Agrivoltaic field monitoring
- Water-energy coordination
- Digital Twin sync

## Demo Role

For the SolarHub demo, NeoCell Agro is presented as the site connection layer behind NOVA Energy and NOVA Agriculture.

## Demo Story

NeoCell connects the agrivoltaic site. NeoAgro forecasts irrigation needs. NeoGrid coordinates energy timing. NOVA Twin simulates the outcome. NOVA recommends an operator action.
